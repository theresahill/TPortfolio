About Me

I began my career in art history because of my intense empathy for artists and a strong desire to know the how and why behind the things that they created. With this education, I loved teaching and finding ways to make art accessible to all kinds of viewers. It is this same impulse of fostering inclusivity and descovering what is at work under the surface that brought me to UX design. When I'm not at my computer, I enjoy attempting to keep my many plants alive, cooking, and expending my extra energy by running and participating in triathlons.
